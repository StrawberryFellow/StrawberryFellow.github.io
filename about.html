About AnimeVerse Con

Welcome to AnimeVerse Con, Ireland's premier celebration of anime, manga, and cosplay culture. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small gathering of passionate fans to one of the most anticipated conventions in the country.

Our Mission

We aim to create a vibrant space where fans can come together to celebrate their love for anime and manga, showcase their creativity through cosplay, and connect with fellow enthusiasts.

What We Offer

  • Professional cosplay competitions
  • Exclusive anime screenings
  • Artist alley featuring local talent
  • Gaming tournaments
  • Panel discussions with industry experts
