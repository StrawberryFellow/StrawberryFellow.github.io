Meet Our Special Guests

We're excited to welcome these amazing voice actors to Animerverse Con!

Yuki Kaji

Voice Actor

Known for: Eren Yeager (Attack on Titan), Meliodas (Seven Deadly Sins), Todoroki Shoto (My Hero Academia)

Yuki Kaji is one of Japan's most prolific voice actors, known for his versatile performances across a wide range of anime series. His emotional portrayal of characters has earned him multiple Seiyuu Awards.

Appearance Schedule:

  • Friday: Opening Ceremony (2:00 PM)
  • Saturday: Autograph Session (11:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
  • Saturday: "Voice Acting Techniques" Panel (4:00 PM)
  • Sunday: Q&A Session (1:00 PM)

Saori Hayami

Voice Actor

Known for: Shinobu Kocho (Demon Slayer), Yukino Yukinoshita (My Teen Romantic Comedy SNAFU), Shoko Nishimiya (A Silent Voice)

Saori Hayami is renowned for her gentle voice and ability to portray a wide range of emotions. Her performances have earned her critical acclaim and a dedicated fanbase.

Appearance Schedule:

  • Friday: Meet and Greet (4:00 PM)
  • Saturday: Autograph Session (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM)
  • Sunday: "Behind the Scenes of Demon Slayer" Panel (11:00 AM)

Mamoru Miyano

Voice Actor & Singer

Known for: Light Yagami (Death Note), Rintaro Okabe (Steins;Gate), Ryuji Sakamoto (Persona 5)

Mamoru Miyano is not just a voice actor but also a successful singer. His distinctive voice has brought many iconic characters to life, and his music career includes multiple hit singles and albums.

Appearance Schedule:

  • Friday: Autograph Session (5:00 PM - 7:00 PM)
  • Saturday: "From Voice Acting to Music" Panel (3:00 PM)
  • Saturday: Mini Concert (8:00 PM)
  • Sunday: Closing Ceremony Performance (5:00 PM)

Kana Hanazawa

Voice Actor

Known for: Ichika Nakano (The Quintessential Quintuplets), Mayuri Shiina (Steins;Gate), Nadeko Sengoku (Monogatari Series)

Kana Hanazawa is known for her sweet voice and ability to play a variety of character types. She's also a successful singer with several albums to her name.

Appearance Schedule:

  • Friday: "Voicing Multiple Characters" Workshop (3:00 PM)
  • Saturday: Autograph Session (10:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
  • Sunday: Fan Q&A Panel (2:30 PM)
