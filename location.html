Convention Location

Animerverse Con will be held at the Grand Convention Center.

123 Main Street
Anytown, USA 12345

Getting Here

By Car

Free parking is available in the convention center parking garage.

By Public Transportation

The convention center is accessible via the Blue Line subway (Grand Station stop) and bus routes 42, 56, and 78.

From the Airport

A shuttle service will be available from City International Airport to the convention center. Shuttles depart every 30 minutes.

Nearby Accommodations

  • Grand Hotel - Adjacent to the convention center (special convention rate available)
  • City Center Inn - 0.5 miles away
  • Comfort Suites - 1 mile away
